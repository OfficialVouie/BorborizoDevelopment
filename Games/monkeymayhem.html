Monkey Mayhem

Chaos, bananas, and survival in the jungle wild.

Monkey Mayhem is a VR game inspired by Gorilla Tag, but with its own chaotic identity. It blends fast-paced movement with unique mechanics, eerie horror elements, and unexpected twists. Whether you're climbing through creepy environments or outrunning bizarre threats, it’s a wild ride full of energy and unpredictability.

  • Developer: Vouie
  • Genre: Survival Action
  • Platforms: Meta
  • Status: In Development
