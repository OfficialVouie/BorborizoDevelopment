Dead Man's Deck

A cursed card game of life, death, and strategy.

A twisted fusion of blackjack and Russian roulette, this roguelike card game puts your fate on the line with every draw. Bluff the undead, outsmart rival outlaws, and press your luck as cursed cards shift the odds in terrifying ways. Each round could bring riches, ruin... or the final pull of the trigger.

  • Genre: Roguelike Card Game
  • Platforms: PC (Steam)
