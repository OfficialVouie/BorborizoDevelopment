Rango

Fast. Fluid. Fatal. This isn’t your average shooter.

Rango is a gritty first-person shooter that delivers intense, fast-paced combat in a raw, immersive environment. Inspired by the ultra-realistic gameplay of Bodycam, Rango dials back just enough realism to keep the action fluid and exhilarating. You’ll engage in brutal gunfights, tactical maneuvering, and high-stakes scenarios that test your reflexes and instincts. With a stylized edge and a focus on responsive controls, Rango balances authenticity with adrenaline — perfect for players who want realism without sacrificing speed and playability.

  • Developer: Vouie
  • Genre: Arena Shooter
  • Platforms: PC
  • Status: In Development
